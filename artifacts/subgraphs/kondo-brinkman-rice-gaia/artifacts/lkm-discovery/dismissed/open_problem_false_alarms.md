| Anderson 1984 finite Stoner enhancement near a Brinkman-Rice/Mott-like fixed point vs Shaginyan 2010 FCQPT condition `1/M* = 0` | dismissed as regime distinction | The Anderson root asserts finite uniform-spin susceptibility enhancement while allowing very large mass renormalization in screened Kondo-lattice heavy-electron compounds. Proximity to an instability is not the same as unscreened ordered local moments in the ground state. |
